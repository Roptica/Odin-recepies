Homemade Pancakes

Ingridients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract(optional)

How to make it:

  1. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  2. Prepare the Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, beat the egg and then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir to combine.

  3. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir with a spoon or whisk until just combined.

    4. Be careful not to overmix, the batter should be a little lumpy.

  4. Heat the Pan: Place a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a small amount of butter to grease it.

    Let the pan heat up for a minute.

  5. Cook the Pancakes: Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot pan for each pancake.

    Cook until bubbles start to form on the surface of the pancake (about 2-3 minutes),

    then flip it and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown on both sides.

  6. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup,

    fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

    7. Enjoy your delicious homemade pancakes!
