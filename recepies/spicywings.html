Spicy wings and mashed potato

Hot and delicious!

This Spicy Chicken Wings with Mashed Potatoes meal is the perfect combination of bold flavors and comfort food.

The crispy, spicy chicken wings are coated in a tangy, flavorful sauce that packs a punch,

while the creamy mashed potatoes provide a smooth and comforting balance.

It’s a simple, yet satisfying meal that’s sure to please your taste buds!

Ingridients:

  • 10 chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

Toss wings in olive oil, hot sauce, paprika, garlic, onion powder, salt, and pepper.

Arrange on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway, until crispy.

Boil potatoes in salted water until tender (15-20 minutes).

Drain and mash with butter, milk, salt, and pepper.

Serve the spicy wings with creamy mashed potatoes for a delicious meal!
