Spagetti Carbonare

Carbonara made easy!

Craving a comforting plate of creamy, savory carbonara? Look no further!

This classic Italian dish is quick, easy, and packed with flavor.

With just a few ingredients and simple steps, you can enjoy an authentic carbonara in no time.

Ready to impress your taste buds? Let's get cooking!

Ingridients:

  • Spagetti (or your favourite pasta)
  • 150g Pancetta or Guanciale
  • 2x Eggs
  • 20g Fresh parsley
  • Two pinches of black pepper
  • Two pinches of salt
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • A splash of love!

How to make it:

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Fill a large pot with enough water to cover the pasta and bring it to a boil over high heat.

    Once the water is boiling, add a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt.

    Add the pasta (400-500g) and cook for 7-8 minutes, or until al dente.

    If you prefer your pasta a bit softer, cook it for a few extra minutes.

  2. Prep the Ingredients: While the pasta is cooking, slice the pancetta into bite-sized pieces.

    Mince the garlic, wash the parsley, and chop it into small pieces.

  3. Make the Sauce: In a separate bowl, grate the cheese. Add the two eggs and mix them with the grated cheese.

    Season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper, then stir everything well.

  4. Fry the Pancetta: In a skillet, saut√© the pancetta over medium heat without adding extra oil. Let it crisp up until golden brown.

    Turn off the heat, then add the minced garlic and chopped parsley. Stir everything together.

  5. Combine Pasta & Sauce: Once the pasta is cooked, drain it (but save a little pasta water).

    Pour the pasta into the skillet with the pancetta mixture.

    Then, immediately pour over the egg and cheese sauce, tossing everything together.

    If needed, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve a creamier consistency.

    6. Your delicious carbonara is ready! Serve it with a side of good wine, beer, or even a refreshing soda.

    Buon appetito!
